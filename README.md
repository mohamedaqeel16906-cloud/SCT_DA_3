# 📊 SkillCraft Technology - Task 3: HR Employee Attrition Dashboard

## 📌 Objective
Build an interactive HR Analytics Dashboard using Microsoft Power BI to analyze employee attrition trends and identify factors influencing employee satisfaction and turnover.

---

## 🛠 Tools Used
- Microsoft Power BI
- Power Query
- DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)

---

## 📈 Dashboard Features

- ✅ Total Employees KPI
- ✅ Employees Left KPI
- ✅ Attrition Rate KPI
- ✅ Average Job Satisfaction KPI
- ✅ Employee Attrition by Age Group
- ✅ Attrition by Overtime Status
- ✅ Average Job Satisfaction by Department
- ✅ Attrition Count by Department
- ✅ Interactive Department & Age Group Slicers
- ✅ Key Insights Section

---

## 💡 Key Insights

- Total Employees: **1470**
- Employees Left: **237**
- Attrition Rate: **16.1%**
- Employees working overtime show higher attrition.
- Research & Development has the highest employee count.
- Most employees belong to the **26–35** age group.
- Average Job Satisfaction is **2.73**.
